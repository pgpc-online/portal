About Us

Inspired by the aspirations and dreams of the youth from Padre Garcia through our visionary leaders: former Congressman and Municipal Mayor Hon. Michael Angelo C. Rivera, incumbent Municipal Mayor Hon. Mayor Celsa B. Rivera, and Municipal Mayor Hon. Vice Mayor Micko Angelo B. Rivera, they took a strong leadership to established the Padre Garcia Polytechnic College (PGPC). Their immeasurable love to serve the disadvantaged sectors of Padre Garcia was enshrined through the Municipal Ordinance No. 42 2023 entitled: ORDINANCE ESTABLISHING THE PADRE GARCIA POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES. The institution served as the strong arm of the Local Government of Padre Garcia to provide globally competitive and high quality education through PGPC academic programs within the reach of the people.

Our Mission

As a higher learning institution in REGION IV, PGPC is committed to produce globally competitive professionals and leaders with industry based knowledge,skills and values that will enable them achieve their professional growth and total human development.

Our Vision

PGPC is a premiere LOCAL higher learning institution in REGION IV producing globally competitive and excellent professionals and leaders instrumental to community development and nation building.
